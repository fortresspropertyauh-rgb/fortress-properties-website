Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Results

Fortress Properties LLC was founded in 2018 with a clear vision: to bring integrity, professionalism, and world-class service to Abu Dhabi’s real estate market. Based in Al Farah Tower on Mina Street, we serve clients across the emirate — from first-time buyers to seasoned investors.

Our team of licensed brokers and consultants specialise in residential sales, leasing, and commercial property across Abu Dhabi’s most sought-after locations — Yas Island, Saadiyat Island, Al Reem Island, Corniche, and beyond.
